Working on Inter-X, a multimodal model for recognizing human behavior from video, text, and audio. I represent pose orientation as an element of SO(3)/SE(3) rather than as a flattened vector, which avoids the averaging and distortion errors that come from treating rotations as ordinary coordinates.
