About My Art

My artistic journey began as a complementary exploration to my scientific work. Through painting, I find a different kind of problem-solving and expression that enriches my understanding of complex systems and patterns.

Each piece represents a moment of creative exploration, often inspired by the mathematical beauty I encounter in my research. The interplay of colors, shapes, and textures mirrors the intricate relationships I study in neural networks and physical systems.

Creative Process

Inspiration

Ideas often emerge from my scientific research, mathematical concepts, or moments of reflection during walks in nature.

Sketching

I begin with rough sketches, exploring composition and color relationships before committing to the final piece.

Creation

The painting process is intuitive yet deliberate, allowing the medium to guide the expression while maintaining artistic vision.

Reflection

Each completed work undergoes careful review, often revealing new insights about the original concept or inspiration.
